Bestee

Innovating the dating app industry with thoughtful user interactions, social support, and security features.

Role—Product Design, Research, Requirements Gathering, Information Architecture, Content Strategy

Overview

Have your ever opened a dating app around your friends? Whether they're single or married, there's always an desire to get involved in someone's dating life.

We collaborated with the Bestee team to support the definition of their business plan, product research, product roadmap, visual design, and interaction concepts. Through a series of discovery sessions, primary and secondary research, and visual design exploration our team quickly put together targeted solutions that enabled Bestee to formalize their business plan and seek initial funding.

The Market

The U.S. dating app market was $3.1 billion in 2020 and is projected to reach $5.5 billion by 2023. While the market is saturated, research has shown that dating app popularity is localized to specific markets with mobile app engagement.

Challenge

The online dating market is saturated with apps that enable singles to create/manage profiles and find potential matches. However, none of these support social interactions (e.g., friends, relatives, etc.), forcing singles to brave online dating alone.

The Opportunity

The team believes that enabling rich social interactions within a dating app takes the anxiety out of online dating, resulting in a better online experience, and improve the likelihood of genuine matches.

Research

We deployed a 21-question survey to the general U.S. population to gauge behaviors and attitudes towards eye exams, purchasing habits, satisfaction with retail and online experiences, and the desirability of a digital solution.

Benchmarking Key Findings

The team worked to compare key features and screens across Tinder, Bumble, and Hinge to understand user expectations and identiy opportunities for disruption.

Safety

Photo verification, flagging inappropriate photos, and the ability to report users aids in supporting the audience feels safe and confident in their matches' authenticity.

Set Your App's Culture

Well-crafted, simple, member principles set the tone for the app. Communicate responsible use of the app and communicate principles that draw audiences in.

Make Connections Easy

Connections are core to the experience. Make that experience as joyful as possible. It's common for users to be able to swipe on “all” users or swipe on only users that have already liked them.

Keep Users in the App

Video chat, voice call, and voice messaging functionality keeps users within the app and allows them to connect with their match more effectively (and verify that their user is who they say they are).

Opportunities

The benchmarking revealed opportunities supporting Bestee's viability in the marketplace. These four items guided stakeholder decisions for feature priority and the project's roadmap.

Empower Connections

The app should use best in class tools to help users sift through the abundance of profiles in a meaningful way.

Social Support

Users looking for love are especially vulnerable. Provide support to users so they feel less alone while dating.

Ease Anxiety

Meeting people who are strangers online can be awkward or even dangerous. Ensure users feel safe and “cared for.”

Enhanced Recommendations

Use collected data, intelligence, and user prpofile data to promote more meaningful connections.

App Design

Design activities were organized into three week-long sprints to rapidly iterate and deliver key screens identified as app differentiators. This cadence enabled the team to move quickly on features and interactions, enable collaboration with stakeholders, and move Bestee's design language forward quickly.

Exploring Matches

Matching is an essential part of any dating app. The mechanics of swiping to dismiss or approve a match have become an industry standard. Bestee layered social tools for sharing, focused on retention of messaging and social support within the bestee app.

Messages

Messages are clearly split into your potential matches and bestees. Potential meatches maintain industry standard messaging, video chat and social tools for exploring matches.

The Bestee messages explore elements of social support with dating within the app, allowing for discussion of potential matches, planning for social events, and platform specific social interaction that provides a rich experience than its competitors.

Systemic intelligence monitors conversations for locations, events, and plans being made. This system presents an opportunity for enhanced security and safety, e-commerce, and data mining.

Security Check

Security tools were top of mind for ensuring that dating app users were as safe as possible. Features like location tracking, automated checkins, and scheduled bestee phone calls were part of the product's roadmap, to ensure their user's safety and to build affinity with the brand.

Enhanced Social Sharing Tools

Bestee explores social support and sharing tools in ways that other dating apps cannot. Features like seamless profile, photo, and message sharing supports enhanced social interactions. In addition to the features shown below, there were planned features for e-commerce tie-ins for outfit planning, and making reservations for date venues.

Conclusion

The engagement with the Bestee team was short and fruitful by design, quickly allowing the team to gain funding in order for next steps.
